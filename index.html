SPAT Task

Instructions

Thank you for participating to the SPAT experiment
The goal of the experiment is to trace the read line as accurately as possible with your cursor
The scoring method is based on the time spent drawing and the accuracy of the drawing
After each trial, your score will appear, to give you an idea of how well you performed
The experiment is composed of 4 blocks, and you will have 1 minute to rest between each block
Best of luck
Alex and Leah
